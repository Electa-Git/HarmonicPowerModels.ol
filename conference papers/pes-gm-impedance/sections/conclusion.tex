\section{Conclusions}
\label{sec:conclusions}

This paper presents a transient invasive method to determine the harmonic network impedance. To this end, a method is presented based the first-order derivative of the positive sequence voltage and current, found using the Taylor-Fourier transform and Fortescue transform. A case study shows the effectiveness of the method beyond the threshold where the relative amplitude of the positive sequence harmonic current phasor is lower than 1\,\% of the fundamental.
